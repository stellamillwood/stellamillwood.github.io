Context & Goal

The research approached waste management as a collective and infrastructural practice and explored how real-time sensor data and coordination tools could support residents in recycling more effectively. The interface was developed from interviews and workshops.

My Contributions

  • Co-facilitated workshops and analyzed interview data
  • Designed the interface and user journey in Figma
  • Developed the web front end with JavaScript, HTML, and CSS
  • Mounted sensors and supported data integration

Research to Design

Workshops highlighted challenges around lack of bin fullness visiblity and disposing of bulky waste, requiring car access. These directly informing the system features.
