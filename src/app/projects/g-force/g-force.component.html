Challenges

The main design challenges revolved around sensory misalignment and accessibility. To create a sense of presence for the user sensory stimuli need to be aligned between the physical and virtual world. For example, when kicking the virtual ball in VR users felt a physical resistance on their foot too (by also kicking a real ball). Also, I redesigned the UI multiple times to improve readability. For example, I increased the font size and changed the opacity of the panel.
