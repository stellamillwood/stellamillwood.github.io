The UI displays metrics for each kick — force, speed, and distance — updating in real time as the ball moves in VR. Pressing "Kick again" resets the metrics for the next attempt.

Virtual view of the ball with hand and foot controllers in VR.

Physical setup showing the ball, controllers, and sensors used to align VR and real-world interaction.

Challenges

The main design challenge was sensory alignment. To support presence, the physical and virtual stimuli needed to match. Users kicked a real ball while seeing a virtual ball move in VR, aligning motion and tactile feedback. I designed the UI with a space-themed visual style and iterated on font size and spacing to maintain readability at different viewing distances — a constraint specific to VR.
